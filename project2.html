How it works

Easy as 1, 2, 3.and you're done!

Step 1:Create Access Key

Access key will be sent to your email address.

Step 2: Create HTML Form

Create a form in your website with our endpoint mode action.

<form action="https://api.web3forms.com/submit" method="POST">
    <input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="YOUR_ACCESS_KEY">
    <input type="text" name="name" required>
    <input type="email" name="email" required>
    <textarea name="message" required></textarea>
    <button type="submit">Submit Form</button>
</form>

Step 3: Add Access Key

Add your access key to start receiving email submissions.
